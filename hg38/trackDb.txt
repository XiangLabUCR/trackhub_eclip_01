#######################
track PB1
compositeTrack on
shortLabel PB1 eCLIP
longLabel PB1 eCLIP IP and Input, plus/minus strands
group default
visibility full
type bigWig
autoScale on
maxHeightPixels 128:32:8
subGroup1 sample IP=IP IN=Input
subGroup2 replicate 1=Rep1 2=Rep2
subGroup3 strand plus=Plus minus=Minus
sortOrder sample=+ replicate=+ strand=+
dimensions dimensionX=replicate dimensionY=strand
dragAndDrop subTracks
priority 1

    # ---- PB1 IN Rep1 ----
